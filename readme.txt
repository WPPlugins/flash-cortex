== Description ==

This plugin provides easy embedding of flash-based videos from various portals and MP3-files (including player) with generation of a corresponding link.

You can copy the full URL of the video and enter it in your post (or page) either by using the quicktag "Flash Cortex" (added by the plugin) or just by typing "[flash]URL[/flash]". The embedded Video will be shown in the preview and of course in the final post (or page).

The resulting (and embedded) source code is fully "XHTML 1.0 Transitional" compliant.

The following 10 video portals are currently supported:

* YouTube.com (all international versions)
* Google (international and german version)
* Sevenload (international and german version)
* Metacafe
* MyVideo
* Vimeo
* Guba
* Yahoo! Video (international version)
* Clipfish
* Revver
* Divshare

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Download the current release
1. Unzip and copy the whole 'flash-cortex' folder to your plugins directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Now you can start to embed videos in your posts / pages.

Update from a plugin-version lower than 1.0.5

1. Download the current release
1. Delete the deprecated folder "flashcortex" in your plugins dirctory
1. Unzip and copy the whole 'flash-cortex' folder to your plugins directory.
1. (Re-)Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Now you can continue to embed videos in your posts / pages.

Update from a plugin-version higher than 1.0.4

1. Download the current release
1. Unzip and copy the whole 'flash-cortex' folder to your plugins directory (and overwrite the existing files).
1. Now you can continue to embed videos in your posts / pages.
